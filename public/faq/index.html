Optimizer About FAQ Owned Charm Builder
What does this optimizer calculate?

It searches armor, charm, and decoration combinations that meet selected skill targets and ranks valid results by defense and slot efficiency.

What is the difference between Charm Mode Off, Suggest After, and Owned?

Off disables charm post-processing. Suggest After adds theoretical RNG charm templates after builds are generated. Owned evaluates your imported owned charm list.

Are suggested RNG charms real charms I own?

No. Suggest After outputs theoretical templates intended to complete missing targets. They are not automatically treated as owned charms.

Can set or group skills be rolled on RNG charms?

No. Set and group skills are excluded from RNG charm suggestions. Only rollable skill types are used for suggested templates.

Why do I get no results?

Typical causes are too many high targets at once, strict armor/deco filters, or near-miss disabled. Try lowering one or two targets, broadening your decoration pool, or enabling near-miss results.

What does Allow Near-Miss Solver Results do?

It keeps builds that are slightly short on requested levels so charm processing can complete them. Near-Miss Max Missing Points controls how short those builds may be.

Why might HIGH charm dependence not appear?

HIGH appears only when a build materially depends on charm completion. If required deficits are already zero before charm help, dependence will be LOW or NONE.

What performance settings are recommended?

Start with default Max Threads (about 80% of available threads) and Max Results Per Thread around 25 to 50. Raise values gradually while watching responsiveness.

How do I use Owned Charm Builder with the optimizer?

Create/import charms in Owned Charm Builder, then set Charm Mode to Owned in the optimizer. Both pages use the same local storage key.

Where does the gear data come from?

The app uses MHDB Wilds API data for armor, armor sets, skills, decorations, and charms.

Does this tool calculate weapon damage?

No. This project is skill-first build planning for armor sets and related setup.
